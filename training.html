Modified For Your Body

Same Room.
Your Version.

One of the biggest differences at Faster Fitness is that coaches do not force one workout version on everyone. If you are newer, coming back after time away, managing an old injury, or ready to push harder, the coach can adjust the session.

Modifications can include exercise swaps, lower-impact options, lighter or heavier loads, shorter ranges of motion, different tempos, extra coaching cues, or a different conditioning target.

Training FAQs

Before Your
First Class.

Answers for people comparing workout classes, group fitness, and personal training near Brentwood and St. Louis.

What happens in a Faster Fitness training session?

Most sessions combine strength work, conditioning, coaching, and smart pacing in a focused 45-minute format. The goal is to train hard with a plan, not wander around guessing.

Is this a group fitness class or personal training?

It is group personal training. You train with others, but the coach still adjusts the workout for your body, ability level, and goals.

What if I have knee, back, shoulder, or mobility limitations?

Tell the coach before class. They can modify exercises, reduce impact, adjust range of motion, or swap movements so the session fits your body.

Do the workouts include strength training?

Yes. Strength training is a core part of the program because it supports fat loss, muscle, confidence, longevity, and everyday function.

How long are the workouts?

Faster Fitness sessions are designed to fit into real life with a focused 45-minute training format.
