Who We Are

Not A Big-Box Gym.
Not A Guessing Game.

Founder And Coaching Standard

Led By Marshall.
Built Around People.

At Faster Fitness, the standard starts with Marshall Ray: coaching should be personal, practical, and specific to the person in front of you. Members should feel seen, challenged, and supported from the moment they walk in.

The goal has always been bigger than harder workouts. It is helping people build strength, confidence, nutrition habits, and a routine they can sustain.

We do not just run workouts. We coach people through real change.

About Faster Fitness FAQs

Know The
Room.

Quick answers for people learning who we are, where we train, and how the coaching works.

Who founded Faster Fitness?

Faster Fitness was founded by Marshall Ray with a focus on expert coaching, accountability, and training that can be adjusted to real people in real life.

Where is Faster Fitness located?

Faster Fitness is located at 1432 Strassner Rd in Brentwood, Missouri, near Clayton, Maplewood, Richmond Heights, Webster Groves, and the I-64 corridor.

Who is Faster Fitness built for?

It is built for people who want structure, coaching, and accountability without having to create their own workouts or feel lost in a big gym.

Do the coaches help beginners?

Yes. Coaches help beginners learn the movements, choose the right workout options, and build confidence without needing to be perfect on day one.
