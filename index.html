Local Fitness FAQs

Before You
Start.

Common questions from people comparing personal training, group fitness, and regular gyms around Brentwood and St. Louis.

What is group personal training at Faster Fitness?

It is a coached 45-minute training session in a small-group setting. You get structure, accountability, and real-time coaching without having to figure out the workout on your own.

Can the workout be modified for my level or injury history?

Yes. Coaches adjust movements, loads, and pacing based on your current fitness level, goals, and injury history so you can train safely and still make progress.

Do I need to be in shape before I start?

No. Faster Fitness is built for real people at different starting points. The coach helps you choose the right version of each workout from day one.

How is Faster Fitness different from a regular gym?

A regular gym gives you equipment. Faster Fitness gives you coaching, a plan, accountability, nutrition support, and a room where someone is paying attention to how you move.
