A Better Training Experience

Faster Fitness was built around a simple belief: people get better results when they are coached, supported, and given a plan they can actually follow. Access to equipment is not enough for most people. Direction matters.

Marshall Ray And The Coaching Standard

Founder Marshall Ray built the studio around education, accountability, and real coaching. His background includes CSCS, PICP Level 2, Precision Nutrition Level 1, and Biosignature Practitioner credentials, but the bigger point is how that education shows up in the room.

Small Enough To Know You

The Brentwood studio is intentionally different from a big-box gym. Coaches learn names, goals, limitations, and patterns. That makes it easier to adjust training and help members stay consistent.

Built For Faster Results

The mission is practical: help people get stronger, leaner, and more confident without making fitness feel like a second job.

How Faster Fitness Applies This

The studio standard is simple: know the member, coach the movement, and make the next step clear.
