Results FAQs

Track The
Right Things.

Helpful answers for people looking for weight loss, body transformation, and body composition coaching in Brentwood.

How does Faster Fitness track results?

Faster Fitness can track progress through coaching check-ins, strength improvements, photos, habits, and InBody body composition scans when appropriate.

What is an InBody scan?

An InBody scan is a body composition assessment that estimates metrics like body fat, lean mass, and segmental muscle balance. It helps show progress beyond the scale.

Are InBody scans required?

No. They are a useful tool for people who want more data, but coaching, consistency, nutrition habits, and training quality still matter most.

How fast will I see results?

Results vary based on starting point, consistency, nutrition, sleep, stress, and training frequency. The goal is sustainable progress, not a quick-fix promise.

Do members lose weight at Faster Fitness?

Many members come for fat loss and body composition change, but the program also focuses on strength, conditioning, energy, confidence, and consistency.
